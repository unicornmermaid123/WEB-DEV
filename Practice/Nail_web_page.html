    Nails!

    By Isabella German

    I will be talking about my passion for doing nails!

    Hello! My name is Isabella and I enjoy doing nails as a hobby! I am 15 years old and I started being creative at a very young age. I loved crafts, using glue, colors, and glitter. I made mini box houses for my Barbie dolls and always got A’s in my art classes. To say the least, I was a very creative child — and I still believe I am today!

    I first started doing nails in 2023 when I was in 7th grade. I would always see my mom doing her nails, so I started as well. To be honest, I wasn’t very good at first! My lines were messy, and I would cut my cuticles so badly that they bled. I would even paint over them to cover it up! Eventually, I got frustrated and gave up, throwing away all my old nail stuff.

    Then I Got Started Again!

    This year, during the summer, I was scrolling on TikTok (like any teen does!) and started seeing lots of videos about nails and nail techs. I thought they were so cool that I began saving them. After seeing more and more videos, I finally did some research and spent my saved money on nail supplies from Amazon. When they arrived, I got straight to work! My first nail set was a summer seashell design in blue. When I went to work, so many people complimented them, and my friends even asked me to do their nails!

    That’s when I had an idea — I could start doing nails for friends and family as a small business! It’s something I truly enjoy, and it lets me show my creativity. One day, I hope to become a real nail tech and maybe even have my own salon.

    Why I Love Doing Nails

    Doing nails helps me relax and express myself. It’s like painting, but on tiny canvases! I love experimenting with colors, patterns, and designs.I feel proud of how much I’ve improved since I first started.
